Bolognese Sauce Recipe

The perfect beef-based sauce for all types of pasta. This recipe can also be used as a sauce for other dishes, such as lasagna and ziti.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat
  2. Add the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. Stir until the vegetables are soft and beginning to brown. (about 8 minutes).
  3. Add the ground beef into the pan, breaking the meat apart as it cooks, until well browned. (about 10 more minutes).
  4. Pour in the red wine, bring to a boil, and cook until the liquid is almost absorbed. (10 to 15 minutes).
  5. Pour in the milk and add the nutmeg. Cook, stirring occasionally, untill the milk is almost absorbed. (10 more minutes).
  6. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, sugar, and oregano. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat to low.
  7. Simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
